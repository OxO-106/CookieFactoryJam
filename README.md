# Cookie Factory Jam
Aidan Liu aidanliu@uw.edu 
Shanti Davis shantid@uw.edu 
Zihan Chen oxo106@uw.edu 

Cookie Factory Jam is a chaotic 3D cooking game where players must fulfill customer cookie orders inside a malfunctioning cookie factory café. Players use a series of unstable machines, including a dough maker, cookie cutter, and oven, to prepare cookies with different shapes and flavors. However, every machine is broken and launches cookies unpredictably across the café. Players must quickly chase down and collect the flying cookies before delivering the correct orders to customers.
